— PRIVACY
POLICY

1. Introduction

Your confidentiality is of paramount importance to us at Kor Klub. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, protect, and manage your personal information across all touchpoints—whether online through our website, mobile app, or third-party booking platforms like Mindbody, or offline during in-studio interactions and events.

Kor Klub is committed to creating a safe, empowering, and transparent wellness environment. This policy reflects our ethical commitment to protecting your privacy and our compliance with applicable data protection laws, including the Information Technology Act (India) and relevant international standards.

This document governs all personal data collected in connection with:

  • Visiting our website
  • Booking and attending classes or events
  • Engaging with us on social media or via email/telephone
  • Making purchases or enrolling in subscriptions
  • Participating in surveys, promotions, or CCTV-monitored studio spaces
2. Information We Collect and When

We collect personal data when you:

  • Create an account or register
  • Book or attend services
  • Purchase items
  • Communicate with us
  • Participate in events or feedback
  • Enter CCTV-equipped premises

Data may include: name, contact details, booking history, payment info, health info, device data, and marketing preferences.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to provide and improve services, contact you, personalize experiences, ensure safety, and comply with legal obligations. Data may also be anonymized for analytics.

4. Who We Might Share Your Information With

We only share your information with trusted partners like:

  • Mindbody Online
  • Razorpay
  • Google Services (Analytics, Ads)
  • Communication platforms
  • Security and IT partners

No data is sold. Sharing is governed by strict contracts and confidentiality.

5. Electronic Communications

We may contact you via email, SMS, WhatsApp, or app notifications. You can opt out of promotional messages but not essential service communications.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance experience, monitor performance, and personalize content. You can manage cookie preferences via your browser.

7. Data Security

We protect your data with encryption, access controls, audits, and physical security (like CCTV). In case of a breach, we will notify you and authorities as required.

8. Data Retention and Disposal

Data is kept only as long as needed or legally required, then securely deleted or anonymized. Retention periods vary by data type.

9. Data Breach Notification & Response

In the event of a breach, we will assess, contain, notify, and report as per DPDP Act, and take corrective measures swiftly.

10. Your Rights, Complaints, and Contact

You have rights to access, correct, delete, object, restrict, transfer, and withdraw consent. To exercise these, contact:
