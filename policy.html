POLICY

Welcome to Kōr Klub, the vision of three close friends who came together with a shared love for movement, design, and the feeling of being fully present in your body.

Located in Jubilee Hills, Hyderabad, the heart of the city, Kōr Klub offers a new-age approach to movement, blending intention with fire. Our classes are built on control, awareness, and breath. They’re designed to challenge the body without overwhelming it.

You’ll feel the difference in your form, your posture, and the way you carry yourself out in the world. We’ve created a studio that feels like a second skin.

The kind of place where you come as you are and leave more in tune with yourself. Here, you’ll find structure, strength, and softness. All held together by community, dialogue, and a shared commitment to move well. This is movement, at the Kōr.
